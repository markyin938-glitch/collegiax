About the Event

This symposium brings together leading physicists and economic theorists to explore the burgeoning intersection of quantum mechanics and financial modeling. As computational power continues to scale, the application of quantum algorithms to market volatility, risk assessment, and resource allocation is becoming a critical area of study for the next generation of researchers.

Join us for a day of intensive panels, peer-reviewed presentations, and networking opportunities designed to bridge the gap between abstract physics and practical economic application.
